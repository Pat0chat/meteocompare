MeteoCompare is the weather app for people who want to understand forecast reliability, not just consult it.

Instead of displaying a single opaque forecast, MeteoCompare queries 8 reference weather models in parallel and shows you where they agree — and where they diverge.

🌍 SUPPORTED MODELS

• AROME France HD (Météo-France, 1.5 km, very high resolution)
• AROME France (Météo-France, 2.5 km)
• ARPEGE Europe (Météo-France, 11 km)
• ARPEGE World (Météo-France, 25 km)
• ICON-EU (DWD, 7 km)
• ICON (DWD, 13 km, global)
• GFS (NOAA, 13 km, up to 16 days)
• ECMWF (European Centre, 25 km)

📊 CONFIDENCE INDEX — THE KEY FEATURE

A 0-100% score tells you how much the models agree:
• Perfect convergence → 95% (you can plan with confidence)
• Strong divergence → 30% (real uncertainty, keep a backup plan)

Weighting favors high-resolution models in their coverage zone (AROME in France, ARPEGE in Europe).

🔒 PRIVACY BY DESIGN

• Zero data collection
• No analytics, no tracking, no ads
• All data stays on your device
• Local-only storage (favorites, cache, preferences)
• Only external requests: Open-Meteo (free, no account)

The app requests exactly two permissions: INTERNET (to query Open-Meteo) and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE (to detect offline mode).
